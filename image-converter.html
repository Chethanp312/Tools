How to Convert Images

  1. Upload your image (drag & drop or click to browse)
  2. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF)
  3. Adjust quality if needed (for lossy formats like JPG/WebP)
  4. Click "Convert Now" and download your converted image

Format Comparison:

  • JPG - Best for photographs (lossy compression)
  • PNG - Best for graphics with transparency (lossless)
  • WebP - Modern format with superior compression
  • GIF - For simple animations (limited to 256 colors)
